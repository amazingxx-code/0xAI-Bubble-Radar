How to read the radar

The radar classifies detected bubble conditions into five levels, ranging from minimal to extreme.

  1. Minimal BubbleVery limited bubble characteristics detected.
  2. Low BubbleBubble pressure remains relatively contained.
  3. NeutralNo clear bubble extreme detected.
  4. Elevated BubbleBubble pressure is becoming more pronounced.
  5. Extreme BubbleExceptional bubble conditions detected.
A high bubble reading does not necessarily imply an immediate price decline. The radar measures bubble conditions, not short-term market timing.
