# 0xAI Bubble Radar

An AI-powered public record tracking bubble conditions across market-leading stocks.

## Reading the scale

The proprietary 0xAI algorithm classifies detected bubble conditions into five levels: Minimal Bubble, Low Bubble, Neutral, Elevated Bubble, and Extreme Bubble.

Each published reading remains in the public archive with its original date, ticker, closing price, and bubble condition.

> A high bubble reading does not necessarily imply an immediate price decline. The radar measures bubble conditions, not short-term market timing.
